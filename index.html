How it works

You stay in control — Codoo does the typing.

A guest writes, Codoo answers from your apartment data, and every conversation is mirrored to your team — so you can step in the moment you want to.

💬

1 · Guest messages

Someone who booked on Booking.com, Airbnb or direct writes on your WhatsApp — the one they already use.

🤖

2 · Codoo answers

It reads the booking and that flat's real check-in, Wi-Fi and parking — and, being an AI, handles the rest, in the guest's language, instantly.

👀

3 · You monitor

Every conversation mirrors to your Telegram group, so the team sees exactly what's happening, live.

✋

4 · Step in anytime

A hot lead, a complaint, or anything Codoo can't answer — reply from Telegram and it steps aside until you hand it back.
