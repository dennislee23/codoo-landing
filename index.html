Setup

Three steps. Your stack stays as it is.

Roland sits on top of what you already use — Beds24, Booking.com, your WhatsApp number. No new app for guests to download.

1

Connect

We plug into your Beds24 / channel manager and your WhatsApp Business number. Nothing changes for your guests.

2

Teach it your flats

Each apartment's check-in, Wi-Fi, parking and rules go in once. Roland keeps them straight per booking, automatically.

3

Hand over the chat

Guests message as normal. Roland answers, and you get a Telegram message only when something needs you.
